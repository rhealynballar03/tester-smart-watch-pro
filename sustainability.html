Our commitments.

Four principles guide every decision, from the first material choice to the day a device retires.

Repairable by design

Standard screws, modular parts and a public teardown guide for every product. Fix it, don't bin it.

Responsible materials

Certified recycled titanium and aluminium, recycled-content plastics, and gold reclaimed from e-waste.

Carbon honesty

We publish the full lifecycle footprint of every device, and offset what we can't yet eliminate.

Take-back & recycle

Send any Tester device back, free, at end of life — we refurbish, harvest parts or responsibly recycle it.
