Returns & Refunds.

Last updated: 1 June 2026

We want you to love what you buy. If you don't, you have 30 days from delivery to return most items for a full refund — no questions asked.

1. The 30-day window

Returns must be requested within 30 days of delivery. Items should be in their original condition and packaging, with all accessories. Light use is fine — we know you need to try a device to know it's right.

2. How to start a return

  • Email returns@testertech.com with your order number (it starts with TT-).
  • We'll send a prepaid return label within one business day.
  • Pack the item, attach the label, and drop it off. Returns shipping is on us.

3. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll refund the original payment method within 5–7 business days. Original shipping charges (where applicable) are refunded for faulty items, but not for change-of-mind returns.

4. Exchanges

Want a different model or size? Start a return and place a new order — it's the fastest way to get the right device to you.

5. Faulty or damaged items

If something arrives damaged or develops a fault, contact us straight away. Faulty items are covered by our 2-year limited warranty and are repaired, replaced or refunded at no cost to you.

6. Non-returnable items

For hygiene and safety, a small number of items (such as opened earbuds' ear-tips) can't be returned once used, unless faulty. This never affects your statutory rights.

7. Questions

Our support team is happy to help — returns@testertech.com or +1 (800) 555-0142, Mon–Fri 9am–6pm PT.
